# Requirements

- You must have a [GitHub account](https://docs.github.com/en/get-started/start-your-journey/creating-an-account-on-github)

# Fork do-exploit/exia-examples to your organization

1. Open https://github.com/do-exploit/exia-examples/fork

2. Uncheck "**Copy the `main` branch only**"

3. Click "**Create fork**"
